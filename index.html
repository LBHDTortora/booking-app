Hotel Tordigliano

4.7
429 votes

Hotel Tordigliano in Positano is highly recommended as an exceptional hotel of great charm and quality. Within five minutes walk of the port and seafront, Hotel Tordigliano has stunning views over the delightful small town of Positano. Meals can be taken al fresco and the swimming pool and terrace also have wonderful views of the Amalfi Coast.

Amalfi, Ravello and Sorrento are within easy reach along with trips to Pompeii, Herculaneum and Ischia.

  • Free airport shuttle
  • Free wifi in all rooms
  • Air conditioning and heating
  • Pets allowed
  • We speak all languages
  • Perfect for families
